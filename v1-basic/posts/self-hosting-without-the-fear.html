Self-Hosting Without the Fear

July 30, 2025
backend

The cloud sells you abstraction, and abstraction is great until you have no idea what is actually running. Self-hosting a single service on a single machine is the fastest way to learn what those abstractions were hiding: ports, processes, reverse proxies, and the unglamorous reality of keeping something online.

Start small. One box, one service, one domain pointed at it. You will break things, and breaking things you own is the cheapest education in backend work you will ever get.
