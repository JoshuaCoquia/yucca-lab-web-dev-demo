excerpt: "Backend-as-a-service gets you shipping fast by borrowing against future flexibility. Know the interest rate."

---

Backend-as-a-service platforms hand you auth, a database, and storage in an afternoon. For a prototype or a solo project, that is an extraordinary deal. The speed is not free, though — you are borrowing infrastructure decisions from a vendor and paying them back in lock-in.

The honest framing is a loan with an interest rate. If the project stays small, you never feel the cost. If it grows, migration off a BaaS is the bill coming due. Neither outcome is wrong; just sign the loan knowing the terms.
