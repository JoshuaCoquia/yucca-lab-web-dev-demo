title: "The Portfolio Is the Proof"
date: "2025-05-19"
tags: ["career"]
excerpt: "A resume claims you can build things. A portfolio shows it. For early-career engineers, shown beats claimed."
slug: "the-portfolio-is-the-proof"
---

Anyone can list technologies on a resume. A portfolio of things you actually shipped — with the source readable and the decisions explained — turns a claim into evidence. For an early-career engineer with a thin work history, that evidence does disproportionate work.

The bar is not polish; it is honesty and reasoning. A small project with a clear writeup of what you chose and why beats a flashy clone with no story. Show the work, show the thinking, and let the proof speak.
